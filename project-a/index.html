What we know so far about Move da Meow?
Move da Meow is a cat toy creature where it doesn’t like to be played with and gets annoyed easily. The movements would be quite random, and likes to inspect the objects on the wood floor, where the eyes turn blue. Its tail swings back and forth almost like a dog, and the main body vibrates subtly.


Guidelines for human interaction with this creature
When the user clicks on the creature once, its eyes will turn red (it gets annoyed), but when clicked on once again, it will run away but it comes back from the left when the space key is pressed. The idea is as if the user is a cat and it’s playing with the toy, but it’s a toy with a mind of its own. It exists in a household environment, on the floor most of the time, and it would interact with the random objects on the floor on its own once in a while.
